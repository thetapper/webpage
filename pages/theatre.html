Theatre

Theatre to me is all about impacting your audience—moving them to feel more deeply and empathize with fictional characters in real-life scenarios.

I've been in theatre productions since I can remember, always loving every moment I get to be on stage and perform or working countless hours in rehearsal.

I see the ghost light many times on stages where I perform, and it reminds me of the importance of theatre in our world. Theatre brings people together, allows us to see life from different perspectives, and challenges us to think critically about the world around us.

As I grow as an actor, singer, and dancer, the finish line keeps getting farther as my desire to get better become greater. There's so much I hope to achieve in the world of theatre, and I can't wait to see where this journey takes me.
